This page explains how RecoverJob sends automated SMS messages on behalf of businesses, how recipient consent is established, and how recipients can opt out at any time.

About the Service

About RecoverJob SMS

RecoverJob is a SaaS platform that sends automated SMS messages on behalf of trades businesses (plumbers, HVAC, electricians, etc.) to individuals who called that business and did not reach anyone. Messages are sent as a direct transactional response to the caller's inbound service request.

Consent

How Consent Works

RecoverJob establishes consent through one of two methods before sending any automated message:

Option 1

Advance Notice — Explicit Consent

Businesses using RecoverJob are strongly encouraged to notify their customers before activation. The notification template states:

"We've partnered with RecoverJob to ensure we never miss your calls. When you call and we're unavailable, you may receive an automated text acknowledgment. Reply STOP to opt out at any time."

Customers who opt out in advance are permanently added to a do-not-text list and never receive automated messages.

Option 2

Inbound Call — Implied Consent

When a customer voluntarily calls a business and does not reach anyone, the call forwards to a RecoverJob-managed number. RecoverJob checks the do-not-text list then sends a single transactional SMS on behalf of the business. The voluntary inbound call establishes an existing business relationship and implied consent for a transactional response under TCPA.
