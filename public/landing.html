Why "RecoverJob"?

Every missed call is a missed job. The average contractor loses 8-15 calls a month to voicemail. RecoverJob texts them back instantly, qualifies them with AI, and sends you everything you need to close the job — while you're still on the ladder.

Simple Setup

How It Works

Up and running in minutes. No tech skills needed.

Customer Calls Your Business Number

They call your dedicated RecoverJob number — the one on your Google profile and ads.

You Miss the Call

You're on a job. No problem. RecoverJob texts the customer back within 15 seconds automatically.

AI Qualifies the Lead

Our AI asks 3 quick questions via SMS — what service they need, how urgent it is, and what type of property. The customer answers on their own time.

You Get a Lead Summary

We text you everything: what they need, urgency, property type, and their number. You call back a warm qualified lead instead of a cold missed call.
