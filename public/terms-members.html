Overview

Service Description

RecoverJob provides an automated SMS review request service for local trades businesses — including plumbers, HVAC technicians, electricians, and similar contractors. After a completed job, businesses use RecoverJob to trigger a two-message flow: first, an opt-in text identifying the business is sent to the customer; second, a Google review link is delivered only if the customer replies YES. Customers who reply STOP or do not respond receive no further messages.

SMS Compliance

Automated Messaging & Consent

RecoverJob uses a two-message SMS flow with explicit keyword opt-in before delivering any review link. When a review request is triggered for a customer, RecoverJob sends a single opt-in message on behalf of the business:

"Hi [Name], this is [Business Name]. We just completed your service. Would you be willing to leave us a quick review? Reply YES for a link or STOP to opt out. Msg and data rates may apply."

A Google review link is sent only if the customer explicitly replies YES. If the customer replies STOP or does not reply, no further messages are sent. Consent is never assumed.
