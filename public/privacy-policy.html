Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026 Current

RecoverJob is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights — including those related to automated SMS messaging sent on behalf of businesses that use our platform.

Data Collection

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information to operate the RecoverJob service:

  • Phone numbers — the business phone number registered with RecoverJob and the phone numbers of individuals who call that business.
  • Call logs — records of missed calls including timestamps, used to trigger automated follow-up messages.
  • Business information — business name, contact details, and account preferences provided at signup.
  • Billing information — payment details processed securely by our payment provider. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Usage data — how users interact with the RecoverJob dashboard, used to improve the product.

SMS Messaging

Automated Text Messages & Consent

RecoverJob sends automated SMS messages on behalf of businesses to individuals who have called that business and did not receive an answer. Before any messaging begins, each business notifies its customers through its outgoing voicemail greeting and advance notice via email, website, or invoices that callers may receive an automated text follow-up. Customers are informed of the SMS program before any message is sent.

Message frequency varies based on call volume. Message and data rates may apply.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any message at any time. Opt-outs are honored immediately and permanently. For help, reply HELP.

Customers may also opt out in advance before receiving any messages, in which case they are added to a permanent do-not-text list.

Phone numbers collected through the RecoverJob platform are never shared with third parties for marketing purposes and are used solely to deliver the automated response service described above.

Full SMS consent disclosure: recoverjob.com/sms-consent

Best Practices

Customer Notification (Recommended)

Businesses using RecoverJob are strongly encouraged to notify their customer base about this service. Proactive disclosure builds trust and reduces opt-out rates. Recommended notification methods include:

  • An email announcement to existing customers
  • A disclosure footer on the business contact page or website
  • A notice on invoices, receipts, or other customer-facing documents

Sample language businesses may use:

"We've partnered with RecoverJob to ensure we never miss your calls. When you call and we're unavailable, you may receive an automated text acknowledgment on our behalf. Reply STOP to opt out at any time."

Data Usage

How We Use Your Information

Information collected through RecoverJob is used exclusively to:

  • Send automated SMS responses to missed callers on behalf of the registered business.
  • Track lead activity and response data within the business dashboard.
  • Process subscription billing and send account-related communications.
  • Improve the reliability and features of the platform.

Data Sharing

We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information — including caller phone numbers — to any third party for marketing or commercial purposes.

We share data only with service providers required to operate RecoverJob (such as SMS carriers and payment processors), and only to the extent necessary to deliver the service. All providers are contractually prohibited from using your data for their own purposes.

Retention

Data Retention

We retain account and business data for as long as your account remains active. Call logs and message history are retained for up to 24 months. Billing records are retained as required by applicable law.

Your Rights

Access, Correction & Deletion

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at the address below. We will fulfill deletion requests within 30 days. Callers may also remove themselves from future messages by replying STOP to any message.

Security

How We Protect Your Data

We use industry-standard safeguards including TLS encryption in transit and encryption at rest. No transmission method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Updates

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated by email or in-app notice. Continued use of the service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
