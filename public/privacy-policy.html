Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

RecoverJob ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our missed-call text-back service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to operate our service:

  • Phone numbers – the business phone number you register and the numbers of callers who reach your business.
  • Call logs – records of missed calls, including timestamps and call duration, used to trigger automated follow-up messages.
  • Business information – your business name, contact details, and account preferences provided during signup.
  • Billing information – payment details processed securely through our payment provider; we do not store full card numbers.
  • Usage data – how you interact with the RecoverJob dashboard (pages visited, features used) to help us improve the product.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to provide and improve the RecoverJob service:

  • Sending automated SMS text messages to callers who missed your business line.
  • Tracking lead activity and response data within your dashboard.
  • Processing your subscription and billing.
  • Sending service-related communications such as account alerts and support responses.
  • Improving reliability, performance, and features of the platform.

We Never Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information or your customers' information to third parties for marketing or any other commercial purposes. Your data is yours.

Data Sharing

We share data only with trusted service providers necessary to operate RecoverJob (such as SMS carriers and payment processors), and only to the extent required to deliver the service. These providers are contractually bound to protect your data and may not use it for their own purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Call logs and message history are retained for up to 24 months. Billing records are retained as required by law.

Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To request deletion of your account and associated data, please contact us at the address below. We will process your request within 30 days.

Callers who received automated texts may also request removal from future messages by replying STOP to any message or by contacting us directly.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest to protect your information. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or via an in-app notice. Continued use of the service after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
